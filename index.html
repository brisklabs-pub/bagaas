ABOUT BAGAAS

Preserving Craftsmanship, Culture, and Community

The word "Bagaas" refers to raw materials traditionally used by Filipino craftsmen in creating handcrafted products, furniture, woven materials, and local artistry. It symbolizes creativity, hard work, sustainability, and the rich heritage of Philippine craftsmanship.

Malinao School for Philippine Craftsmen (MSPC) was founded to empower students through practical skills, innovation, and hands-on education rooted in Filipino values and culture. The institution continues to nurture future craftsmen, leaders, and professionals through modern education and technical excellence.

Guided by discipline, creativity, and community collaboration, the school promotes lifelong learning while preserving the identity and traditions of local craftsmanship for future generations.
